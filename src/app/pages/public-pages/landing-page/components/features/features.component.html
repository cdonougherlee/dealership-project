Features

Want an electic car with a backup plan? We've got you covered. Our plug-in system ensures that your CO2 emissions are kept minimal by allowing most of your everyday journeys to be fully electric, while also allowing you to go on long road trips without range anxiety. You can choose from multiple driving modes, making sure you always have the power delivery you want. Featuring regenerative braking and energy consumption tracking, your drivetrain is on the cutting edge of technology.

All-wheel drive (AWD) means the car is driving and monitoring all four wheels at the same time, all the time. This improves traction and safety, whist also improving the power and off-road capabilities of the car. Our AWD systems will garantee your ready for any driving conditions that come your way, while avoiding the technical complexity and weight a traditional 4WD system brings on.

Our collision avoidance function helps reduce the risk of incidents caused by driver error such as drifting out of lanes. The car will guide itself back between the lines by itself without driver input, aided by 360 sensors all around the car. The system is run on the world renound Unity engine, known for its award winning computer vision capabilities.

We are confident that our cars are built to last, however unforeseen manufacturing issues can occur. That's why with any Volvo purchase, you can have peace of mind that any issues will be covered with our 5-year unlimited milage warranty. Any and all defects will be completely remedied free of charge by our in house Volvo specialist technicians, and of course with 100% genuine OEM parts. Even if you sell your Volvo before the warranty expires, the warranty will be transferred to the new owner, ensuring a smooth and painless transaction.

Offering comfort, safety and utility, the S90 wagon will be everything you know and love, with more.
